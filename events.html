Come Hang Out With Us

You know that Red Card Pub is the place to come hang out after work and enjoy a nice beer or cocktail with your Tex Mex fare. Did you know that we also host events?

Catch the Game

We make sure to play all the big games on our big screen TVs. We have one always up in the indoor area, and another that we put out on the patio during good weather. When we say big games, we mean WSU sports, Major League Soccer (Go Seattle Sounders!), USMNT, USWNT, Liverpool, and the Dallas Cowboys.

Play Your Own Game

We've got physical games to entertain you and your friends. Sure, this might not be an "event," but hey, it's something to do. We've got cards, connect-4, binho, and more.

We'll see you soon!
