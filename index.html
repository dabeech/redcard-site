Come for the Tex Mex, Stay for the Fun

Here at Red Card Pub & Grub, we've got it all as long as you don't expect too much. We've got beer. We've got margaritas. We've got Tex-Mex. Have you ever heard of a whiskey-rita? Yeah, we've got that too. This is the kind of place you go to vent some steam after a long day at the office. A chill, no-frills atmosphere with chill, no-frills people. Does that sound good to you? Then come on in!
